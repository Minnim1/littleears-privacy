Privacy Policy

We believe your privacy is a right, not a feature.

Last updated: May 2026

🛡️

What we collect

We collect only your email address and the date you created your account. This information is used solely to authenticate you and keep your session active. We do not collect names, phone numbers, location data, or any other personal information.

🎙️

Voice recordings

Voice recordings you make within the app are stored exclusively on your device. They are never uploaded to our servers, never shared with third parties, and never leave your phone. You can delete them at any time from within the app.

☁️

Stories & content

Story content (text and audio narration) is stored on our secure servers (Supabase) to deliver the app experience. We do not sell, share, or monetize this content in any way.

👶

Children's privacy

Little Ears is designed for children aged 2–10, used under parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Accounts are managed by parents or guardians. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us immediately at privacy@littleears.app and we will delete it promptly.

📊

Analytics & tracking

We do not use third-party analytics, tracking SDKs, or advertising networks. We do not track your behavior across apps or websites. We do not sell your data. Ever.

🗑️

Delete your data

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at privacy@littleears.app. We will process your request within 30 days in accordance with applicable law.

🔒

Security

All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Your password is never stored in plain text — we use Apple Sign In or secure hashed email authentication via Supabase Auth.

🍪

Cookies

The Little Ears mobile app does not use cookies. If you visit our website, only essential session cookies may be used — never for advertising or tracking purposes.

🔄

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact us

For any privacy questions, data deletion requests, or concerns:
privacy@littleears.app

We aim to respond to all privacy requests within 7 business days.
